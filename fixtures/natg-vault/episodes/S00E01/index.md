The [[uss-testament]] arrived at [[Starbase Kappa]] under orders from [[Admiral Placeholder]] to quietly investigate Syndicate traffic routing through [[The Wayward Comet]]. [[commander-val-thelor|Commander Thelor]] led the shoreside team.

![[station-map.svg]]

Their initial contact with [[Vex]] at the bar was civil but evasive; by the end of the first evening, the team had turned up enough inconsistencies in transit manifests to suggest that something more than black-market pharmaceuticals was moving through the promenade. See the [[handouts/captains-log|captain's log]] handout for Thelor's own account of the first night.
