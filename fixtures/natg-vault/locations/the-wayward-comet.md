A dim, cramped bar tucked into the Promenade on [[Starbase Kappa]]. The Wayward Comet is known primarily as a meeting point for the fringes of civilian shipping and, less openly, as a recruiting ground for the [[Kappa Syndicate]].

Regulars include [[npcs/vex|Vex]], whose tenure behind the bar predates the current station administration.
