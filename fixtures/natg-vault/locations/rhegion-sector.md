A frontier sector on the Federation's trailing edge. The Rhegion Sector is nominally Federation space, patrolled by [[Starfleet]] units operating out of [[Starbase Kappa]] in the [[Vell Prime System]]. Its aging infrastructure and thin patrol coverage have made it attractive to organizations like the [[Kappa Syndicate]].
