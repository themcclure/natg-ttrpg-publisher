Starbase Kappa is the Federation's administrative and logistical anchor in the [[Rhegion Sector]]. It hosts [[Admiral Placeholder]]'s command staff, extensive repair bays, and a civilian promenade that includes [[The Wayward Comet]].

On any given day, [[Vex]] is likely holding court behind the bar there, regardless of what shift rotation Starfleet thinks is running.
