# Dominion

**Type:** Government (Founder-led totalitarian empire)
**Headquarters:** Founders' homeworld, Gamma Quadrant
**Status:** Active — withdrawn behind the Bajoran Wormhole; no Alpha Quadrant presence

---

## Overview

Gamma Quadrant totalitarian empire. Founders are Changelings — shapeshifters. Administered through Vorta; enforced by Jem'Hadar (addicted to ketracel-white). War ended Treaty of Bajor 2375; Dominion withdrew.

The Dominion is not here. They're behind the wormhole. But what they did is present in everyone who lived through the war.

The fear of Changeling infiltration — anyone could have been a Founder — was real, demonstrated, and did lasting damage to institutional trust. Formal peace has not erased it. Any Jem'Hadar encountered in the Alpha Quadrant post-war is a rogue unit, cut off from white supply. That is a specific kind of dangerous.
