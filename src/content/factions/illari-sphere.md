# Illari Sphere

**Type:** Government (internal structure entirely unknown)
**Headquarters:** [[isolationist-system]] (location known; interior entirely unknown)
**Status:** Active — total isolationists; heliopause marker network maintained

---

## Overview

The Illari have enforced total external isolation for what is believed to be 200–300 years. Their border is continuously monitored; every arriving vessel receives an automated message in every known sector language:

> *"We are the Illari. We do not wish to know you, and you may not know us. Leave, immediately."*

Ships that don't leave are escorted out — or, reportedly, destroyed.

The message broadcasts in every language the Illari have intercepted from sector traffic. They have been listening longer than anyone realizes.
