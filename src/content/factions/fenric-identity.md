# Fenric Identity

**Type:** Government
**Headquarters:** Fenric III, [[fenric-system]]
**Status:** Active — cautiously interested in the outpost; reclusive overall

---

## Overview

A heavy-bodied mustelid-descended species who occupy the strategic heart of the Persephone Sector — Fenric's Star, the Geometry junction through which all major transit must pass. They have spent approximately a century as a proxy battlefield between the Kheth Confederacy (raiders) and the Varanid Authority (arms supplier and trade dependency arrangement), and are tired of it. Not aggrieved. Tired and pragmatic.

They maintain a strong tradition of non-interference in others' affairs and expect the same. Their system's position at the Geometry's central junction makes them strategically significant to all parties. Relations with the outpost are believed to be cordial and should be maintained carefully.

---

## Species: Fenric

Heavy-bodied mustelid-descended endotherms with semi-aquatic ancestry. Communal by deep instinct — solitary Fenric are culturally anomalous. Long-lived.

Domestic architecture emphasizes communal space over private quarters. Decisions are made collectively — "I'll need to discuss that" is not evasion, it is how the Fenric government actually functions.

A Fenric can assess the physical state of substances, structures, or machinery through direct sensory contact — structural integrity, material composition, mechanical condition. This is Environmental Sensitivity; it is not metaphysical. It is biology.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Insight +1, Fitness +1, Presence +1. Trait: *Rooted* — excels at community coordination, environmental reading, and endurance. Disadvantaged when separated from community. Ability: *Environmental Sensitivity* — once per scene, ask the GM one factual question about the physical state of a substance, structure, or machine the Fenric is in direct contact with.

---

## Outpost Presence

The Fenric maintain a diplomatic suite in [[outpost-428]]'s Diplomatic Wing. Relations are described as cordial. Any outside offer from the Fenric gets read carefully for what it costs them in independence — the Varanid Authority taught them what a "good deal" looks like.

A multi-generational Kheth community has been established on Fenric II for decades. First-generation retain Confederacy cultural identity; later generations are fully Fenric Identity. This creates periodic friction with the Confederacy.

---

## Key Relationships

- **Federation:** Cautiously interested; no formal alliance
- **Varanid Authority:** Trade relationship; arms supply in exchange for commercial concessions. The Fenric are aware of the dependency dynamic.
- **Kheth Confederacy:** Raider activity attributed to affiliated Kheth clans; refugee community on Fenric II creates both a bond and a complication
