# Orion Syndicate

**Type:** Criminal organization (distributed, cell-based)
**Status:** Active — believed present in sector; identity at the outpost unconfirmed

---

## Overview

One of the largest criminal organizations in the Alpha Quadrant. Distributed cell structure makes decapitation strategies ineffective. Operations: smuggling, extortion, information brokering, piracy, labor trafficking. Works through legitimate-seeming fronts — trading company, labor contractor, hospitality business.

The Syndicate doesn't announce itself. Presence is inferred from patterns: cargo that moves without a paper trail, debts that get paid in ways that don't add up. Violence is a business cost, not a preference. Escalation means someone decided you were worth it.

> [!NOTE] A crime syndicate is believed active in the Persephone sector. The Orion Syndicate is the most plausible candidate but has not been confirmed at the outpost.
