# Nausicaan

**Type:** Species / loose mercenary culture (no unified government)
**Status:** Active — present throughout the Alpha Quadrant as individuals and small groups

---

## Overview

No unified government; individual mercenaries, pirates, and hired muscle operating throughout the Alpha Quadrant. Big, aggressive, direct — subtlety reads as weakness. Violence is a normal social register, not an escalation.

A Nausicaan on a trade outpost is almost certainly doing a job for someone. Finding out who matters more than the Nausicaan themselves. Individual Nausicaans can be honorable within their own code — a contract fulfilled is a contract fulfilled.

No established presence at the outpost at campaign start.
