# Starfleet

**Type:** Combined military, scientific, and exploratory service of the [[federation]]
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, Earth
**Status:** Active — post-war rebuilding; drawdown in progress at frontier postings

---

## Overview

Federation's combined scientific, exploratory, and defence force. At Outpost 428, Starfleet personnel serve on secondment under FORA authority — they are supporting the outpost, not running it. The chain of command runs to the Director.

Post-war Starfleet is stretched: fewer ships, fewer personnel than five years ago. The drawdown at this outpost is visible to sector powers and is being read as a signal about Federation frontier commitment. The PCs' arrival — with Starfleet credentials and a new runabout — is the first such rotation in some time. Both details have been noticed.

Starfleet personnel rotate; civilians with decades at the outpost have seen many rotations. They are polite about it.

---

## Shift Structure at the Outpost

The overnight shift (the PCs) are the newest, most junior rotation. Day shift carries the most experienced personnel and handles the bulk of scheduled trade and diplomatic traffic. The overnight shift has maximum autonomy and minimum oversight — and is the flexible resource the Director deploys on away missions and off-outpost work.
