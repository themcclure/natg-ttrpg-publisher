# Federation

**Type:** Government (multi-species liberal democracy)
**Headquarters:** Paris, Earth (Federation Council); San Francisco, Earth (Starfleet Command)
**Status:** Active — post-war rebuilding

---

## Overview

Multi-species democratic republic spanning 150+ member worlds. Administers Outpost 428 through FORA (Federation Office of Regional Administration) — a civilian body with a mandate distinct from Starfleet Command.

At this posting, the Federation Council is 85 days away by dispatch. "What would the Federation want?" is a question the crew answers for themselves, in real time, with imperfect information.

Post-war, the Federation is rebuilding and the certainty of the pre-war years has taken some hits. Frontier commitments are under pressure — the Starfleet drawdown at Outpost 428 is a symptom of broader policy conversations happening 85 days away with no live relay.

## Notes

FORA is the Federation on this outpost, not Starfleet. The chain of command runs to the Director. Starfleet personnel serve on secondment to support the outpost's mandate, not to run it.
