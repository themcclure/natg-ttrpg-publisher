# Breen Confederacy

**Type:** Government (Confederacy — internal structure opaque)
**Headquarters:** Breen homeworld (location not publicly known)
**Status:** Active — post-war; no active Alpha Quadrant presence confirmed

---

## Overview

Internal structure, homeworld, and biology all unknown or deliberately obscured. Nobody knows what a Breen looks like; the refrigeration suits are always on — whether physiological or cultural is unknown.

Joined the Dominion War late (2375), contributing energy dissipator technology that disabled Federation shields and struck Starfleet HQ on Earth. Signed no post-war peace treaty equivalent to the Treaty of Bajor — cold coexistence with the Federation. Not in active conflict; no formal peace framework.

The Breen made a bet on the Dominion and it lost. What they're doing with that outcome is not publicly known.
