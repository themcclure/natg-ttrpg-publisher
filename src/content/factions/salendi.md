# Salendi

**Type:** Colonial government (structure unknown)
**Headquarters:** Salend system (homeworld name unknown to Federation records)
**Status:** Active — pre-warp; no Federation contact established

---

## Overview

The Salendi are the inhabitants of a world in the [[salend-system]]. They are human in appearance but do not identify as human or claim any off-world origin — they identify entirely by their world's name. No Federation contact has been established. They have no warp capability.

The Varanid Authority is believed to maintain a commercial presence in the system. The nature of that relationship from the Salendi side is unknown.

> [!NOTE] The physical resemblance between the Salendi and humanity has not been explained by any available source. Parallel evolution, shared ancestry, or another explanation have not been confirmed.

---

## Key Relationships

- **Varanid Authority:** Believed to have an established commercial relationship
- **Federation:** No contact; Prime Directive considerations apply
