A loose criminal network that takes its name from the starbase whose shipping lanes it preys on. The Kappa Syndicate deals primarily in black-market medical supplies and forged transit papers; whether it has leadership in the conventional sense is a matter of active debate among [[Starfleet]] intelligence analysts.

Known points of contact include [[The Wayward Comet]] on [[Starbase Kappa]].
