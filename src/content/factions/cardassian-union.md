# Cardassian Union

**Type:** Government (civilian Detapa Council, post-war reconstruction)
**Headquarters:** Cardassia Prime
**Status:** Active — severely degraded; ~300–450 ly away

---

## Overview

Post-war Cardassia is a wounded civilization, not a threat. ~800 million dead; Obsidian Order destroyed; military broken. The Detapa Council is governing a traumatised, depopulated world. Any Cardassian in this sector is operating on personal initiative — not state policy.

The Obsidian Order (Cardassian intelligence service) was effectively destroyed: hollowed out by the 2371 Omarion Nebula disaster, finished by the war. What existed as Cardassian sector intelligence assets before 2371 may still be running in the field — stale, unsupported, with no handler to report to.

Individual Cardassians are not the Cardassian Union. The gap has never been wider.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Presence +1, Reason +1. Ability: *Healthy Suspicions* — spend 1 Threat to ask if an NPC is lying; GM must answer honestly.
