# Kelvari Reflection

**Type:** Government (structure unknown — believed communal or council-based)
**Headquarters:** [[kelvar-system]] (~6 light-years from Varanidia)
**Status:** Active — rediscovered ~100 years ago; commercial negotiations with Varanid Authority ongoing

---

## Overview

A Varanid offshoot colony that departed Varanidia centuries ago to build a civilization organized around creative output and intellectual exploration rather than trade leverage. At warp 1 capability, the six-year voyage to their new system was enough to be left alone. When warp 2 made the crossing viable approximately 100 years ago, someone made the trip and found a thriving, distinctly different sub-species that had no interest in being reintegrated.

They will talk. They won't be absorbed.

The Varanid Authority has maintained commercial interest in the system since re-contact — the system's geothermal mineral wealth and asteroid belt resources are significant. Negotiations for trade agreements are ongoing; the Kelvari have not signed.

---

## Species: Kelvari

Same body plan as Authority Varanid — ectothermic humanoid. Visually similar; culturally diverged beyond easy reintegration. The geothermally warm homeworld means they are metabolically comfortable without stimulants; posture is more relaxed and open than Authority Varanids.

The central cultural rite is the reflecting pools: sitting at a still geothermal pool staring into your own reflection until there are no secrets you're keeping from yourself. The name "Kelvari Reflection" carries this literal meaning.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Presence +1, Insight +1 (or cultural variant; confirm before play).

The Kelvari Reflection maintains a diplomatic presence on [[outpost-428]]. Per the crew briefing, relations are described as active but smaller than the Varanid Authority's establishment.
