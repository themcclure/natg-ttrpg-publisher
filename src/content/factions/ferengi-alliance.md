# Ferengi Alliance

**Type:** Government (Grand Nagus-led commercial oligarchy)
**Headquarters:** Ferenginar
**Status:** Active — commercially intact post-war

---

## Overview

Commercially-driven society governed by Grand Nagus Rom. Declared neutrality in the Dominion War and maintained it — making them one of the few Alpha Quadrant powers with commercial infrastructure intact in 2377. Everything is a transaction.

A Ferengi on a sector-edge trade outpost with multiple civilizations in commercial contact is not surprising. A Ferengi who won't say why they're specifically here is worth watching.

Post-Grand Nagus Rom, the Alliance is in a period of cautious social reform. A Ferengi's position on the reforms is a quick character-defining question.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Insight +1, Presence +1. Ability: *The Great Material Continuum* — once per session, reduce any task's Opportunity Cost by 1. Also: immune to telepathy/empathy (four-lobed brain); disease-resistant.
