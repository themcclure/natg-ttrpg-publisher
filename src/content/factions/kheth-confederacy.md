# Kheth Confederacy

**Type:** Government (loose clan confederation)
**Headquarters:** Kheth Prime, [[gj-1847-system]]
**Status:** Active — outpost-resident clan (Clan Fortate) for 30 years; outside clans are a separate matter

---

## Overview

A clan-based society of endothermic centauroids from a resource-depleted binary star system. Each clan owns and operates ships; loyalty runs to clan first, Confederacy second. A unified response requires inter-clan consensus, which takes time — and is not guaranteed.

A long-established Kheth clan has maintained a continuous presence on Outpost 428 since its founding 30 years ago and should be considered permanent residents, not rotating diplomatic staff. They are invested in the outpost's stability more than any rotating Starfleet secondee.

Be aware that a Kheth standing upright on their rear legs is adopting a formal or assertive posture — not a threat display. Sudden loud sounds trigger an involuntary freeze-or-scatter reflex; don't shout for emphasis.

## Species: Kheth

Four-legged centauroids approximately four feet long, with two manipulator arms, large predator eyes, and large erect ears. Standing upright is formal or assertive, not an attack posture.

Two-tier naming: a public name used with outsiders, and a private in-clan name earned through demonstrated worth, not shared with non-Kheth.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Fitness +1, Daring +1, Control +1. Trait: *Clan-Born* — deep loyalty to clan; complication when clan interests diverge. Ability: *Never Flat Footed* — never surprised by ambush; add d20 to detect concealed threats. Optional Talent: *Field Expedient* — reduce Difficulty by 1 when repairing or modifying existing equipment.

## Outpost Presence

**Clan Fortate** are the Kheth resident clan on Outpost 428. A Kheth clan took the diplomatic posting when the outpost opened ~30 years ago and named themselves after the outpost — *Fortate*, derived from "Outpost four..." heard in Standard, rendered through Kheth phonology. The name was chosen before FORA formally acknowledged their permanent residency. The clan considers that gap irrelevant. They had already decided.

Three generations are now in residence: founding elders who remember the vessel they came from, second-generation who run the current diplomatic function, and the youngest cohort who have never known anything else.

Beyond the diplomatic function, Kheth from Clan Fortate are embedded throughout the outpost's service and maintenance workforce — maintenance, docking operations, cargo handling, security support. This is cultural: you maintain your ship. The practical result is that their access and institutional knowledge extends into every corner of the station's physical infrastructure.

## Key Relationships

- **Federation:** Diplomatic relationship through Outpost 428; generally cooperative on commercial matters
- **Fenric Identity:** Complex — Kheth refugee community is established on Fenric II; raider activity in the Fenric system attributed to affiliated (though technically unaffiliated) Kheth clans
- **Varanid Authority:** Competing interests; the Confederacy wants trade routes and the star at Fenric's Star
