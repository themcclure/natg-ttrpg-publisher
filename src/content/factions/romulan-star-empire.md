# Romulan Star Empire

**Type:** Government (imperial, Senate-based with Praetor authority)
**Headquarters:** Romulus
**Status:** Active — ~400–600 ly away; intelligence assets possibly active in sector

---

## Overview

Authoritarian imperial state with a significant intelligence apparatus (the Tal Shiar) that was severely degraded by the 2371 Omarion Nebula disaster and further depleted by the Dominion War. Post-war Romulans fought alongside the Federation — that did not make them friends.

Sector distance makes direct military reach implausible. Any Romulan presence in this sector is intelligence assets and intermediaries only. Assets may be operating without active handlers — running on orders from 2371 from people who are now dead.

Nothing a Romulan says is the whole truth. Assume layers.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Fitness +1, Reason +1. Ability: *Paranoia* — any adversary acting before them costs the GM 1 extra Threat.
