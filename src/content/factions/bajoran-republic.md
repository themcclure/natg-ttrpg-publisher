# Bajoran Republic

**Type:** Government (democratic republic, faith-integrated governance)
**Headquarters:** Bajor
**Status:** Active — post-occupation reconstruction; Federation membership imminent or recent

---

## Overview

50-year Cardassian occupation ended 2369. Bajor emerged damaged and immediately became strategically central via the Bajoran Wormhole — the only stable passage to the Gamma Quadrant. Civil and religious authority share political space: First Minister and Kai. The Prophets are not merely theological; they interact with Bajoran political life in ways that have produced verifiable guidance.

The occupation lasted over 50 years. It is not the past to Bajorans — it is recent, lived, and present. Any Cardassian in the room changes the air.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Daring +1, Insight +1. Ability: *The Will of the Prophets* — gains 1 Determination when the GM spends 3+ Threat at once.
