# Klingon Empire

**Type:** Government (imperial, Chancellor-led High Council)
**Headquarters:** Qo'noS
**Status:** Active — post-war position of relative strength; no active presence in the Persephone sector

---

## Overview

Warrior culture governed by Chancellor Martok and the High Council post-war. Fought effectively in the Dominion War; emerged with territory and honour intact.

No active Klingon presence in the Persephone sector. House politics matter — any Klingon NPC's House affiliation shapes their position. Directness is respected. Diplomatic hedging reads as weakness or dishonesty.

The only established Klingon presence on Outpost 428 is the unnamed cook of the Klingon Restaurant in the Persephone Agora — see [[outpost-428-public]]. Backstory is a matter of lively outpost rumor.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Daring +1, Fitness +1, Presence +1. Ability: *Brak'lul* — 1 point Protection (always active) + re-roll First Aid task.
