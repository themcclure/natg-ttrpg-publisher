# Varanid Authority

**Type:** Government
**Headquarters:** Varanidia Prime, [[varanidia-system]]
**Status:** Active — primary commercial partner; long-established diplomatic presence on the outpost

---

## Overview

An ectothermic humanoid trade empire built through commercial relationships rather than conquest. Trade is both their primary instrument of diplomacy and their cultural center of gravity. The Varanid Authority has maintained a diplomatic presence on Outpost 428 since the outpost's early years, and commercial relationships with the Federation in this sector are the most well-developed of any pocket empire.

They are reliable partners and expect to be treated as equals. They should be.

The Authority is self-sufficient in resources, which means their traders operate from a position of comfort rather than desperation. They can afford to walk away, and they know it. Negotiations proceed on their timescale.

---

## Species: Varanid

Fully ectothermic humanoids from a warm, humid G6V world. Patient, precise, attuned to social signals. Metabolically slower in cold environments. 

Clothing is entirely status language — material, cut, and color communicate wealth, family line, and current standing. An observer who knows the code can read the room without a word being spoken.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Presence +1, Insight +1. Trait: *Cold-Blooded Patience* — excels at composure, extended negotiation, waiting. Liability in cold environments. Ability: *Beneath the Display* — once per scene, ask the GM the target's most pressing unspoken need or desire.

---

## Outpost Presence

The Authority maintains a full diplomatic suite in [[outpost-428]]'s Diplomatic Wing. **Navar** is the current Varanid Authority ambassador — has been at the station for several years, established, known to the regular crew. Genuinely warm; also never off the clock.

---

## Key Relationships

- **Federation:** Well-established commercial and diplomatic relationship
- **Kheth Confederacy:** Competing interests; the Authority wants trade routes the Kheth want to control or tax
- **Fenric Identity:** Varanid trade concessions in the Fenric system; ongoing commercial influence
- **Kelvari Reflection:** Authority has re-established contact ~100 years ago; commercial negotiations ongoing
- **Illari Sphere:** Believed to maintain back-channel trade via third-party intermediaries — neither side acknowledges it
