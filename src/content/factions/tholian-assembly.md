# Tholian Assembly

**Type:** Government (structure largely unknown)
**Status:** Active — adjacent to sector; watching

---

## Overview

One of the oldest and most reclusive powers in the region. Silicon-based, high-temperature environment — all contact is remote; no Tholian has ever been in the same room as a Federation diplomat. Intensely territorial: any incursion into claimed space triggers a formal response, then physical enforcement. They do not repeat themselves. If a boundary has been stated, it has been stated.

The previous Director of Outpost 428 (a joined Trill) was originally posted to this sector specifically for Tholian negotiation experience. Gareth Desar has had to manage the boundary relationship without that expertise.

Tholian Webs are real. Don't be slow about leaving.

---

## Notes

The Assembly is believed adjacent to the Persephone Sector. The Persephone Geometry's complexity near the sector's outer edge may create navigational situations where a course passes closer to Tholian-claimed space than charts indicate. Plan accordingly.
