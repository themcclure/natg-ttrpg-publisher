# Vulcan

**Type:** Federation Member State (civilian government — Vulcan High Council)
**Status:** Active — Federation member; no direct sector presence

---

## Overview

Federation member state; oldest and most politically influential non-human member. Emerged from the Dominion War intact and with significant institutional continuity.

A Vulcan's emotional life is present and real — it is controlled, not absent. They feel everything; they choose not to display it. Logic is an ethical framework, not just a cognitive one. Decisions are explained; unexplained action implies something is being concealed.

**Species mechanics (STA2e):** Control +1, Insight +1, Reason +1. Ability: *Kolinahr* — resist emotional manipulation; bonus to tasks resisting coercion or deception attempts.
