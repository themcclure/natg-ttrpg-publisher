Commander Val Thelor serves as First Officer aboard the [[uss-testament]] under the broader umbrella of [[Starfleet]]. Before the events of the pilot, Thelor had completed two tours in the Rhegion Sector and was known to [[npcs/admiral-placeholder|Admiral Placeholder]] personally.

Thelor is cautious by temperament and tends to defer diplomatic judgement calls upward, though the events at [[The Wayward Comet]] put that reflex to the test.
