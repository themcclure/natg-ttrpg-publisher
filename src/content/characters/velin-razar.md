## Background

Grew up on a smuggler crew — deep-space practical before he was Starfleet theoretical. Joined the service on the Command track; early in his career was assigned to a survey posting charting incomplete regions, which turned out to suit him better than most conventional postings. He came back with a stellar cartography citation and a clearer sense of why he'd put on the uniform.

He was also present for — or compared notes with [[fiona-glenn]] closely enough to share — an encounter that didn't resolve cleanly. Some kind of anomaly or event that registered wrong and then didn't. He still carries the open question.
