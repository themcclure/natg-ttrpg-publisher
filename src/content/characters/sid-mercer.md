## Background

Grew up on starships and starbases — Starfleet was the context before it was ever a choice. Son of Admiral Mercer; the family name carries weight in the service. His own Academy career ended in a quietly managed exit after an ethics violation, which is a matter of record for anyone who looks. He's been working as a contractor since — trade background, commercial experience, a practical understanding of how things move and who moves them.

He was mentored early in his career by someone who shaped his approach to the work; the shape of that influence is visible in how he operates. He's also been in a situation where command fell to him unexpectedly and he had to make it work. Both events are his to elaborate on.

He arrived at Outpost 428 a few months ahead of the current crew. The posting framed itself as an opportunity; he's been treating it that way.
