## Background

Raised on her homeworld; creative upbringing. Early in her Starfleet career served as a cultural liaison on a formal exchange assignment to a non-Federation world — extended immersion in a society organized on fundamentally different principles. She found the cultural distance less disorienting than most participants, which was revealing. She came back with her diplomatic values crystallised and her Diplomacy focus sharpened by having to use it where Federation assumptions didn't apply and Federation rank didn't open doors.

Later in her career she identified a key leverage point in a hostile or politically fraught situation — finding the crack in the problem and using it, when a harder and faster path was available and she chose not to take it.
