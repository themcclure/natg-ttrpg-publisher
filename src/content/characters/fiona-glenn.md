## Background

Raised in a Starfleet environment; the service was the shape of her world before she chose it for herself. Early in her career she lost someone close to her — the specifics are her own, but the aftermath is visible: a Value about finding herself far from home, and a stubborn insistence that where there is life, there is hope, which implies she's argued herself back from the opposite position at least once. Whatever it was, it's part of why she's here rather than somewhere easier.

Later in her career she shared an encounter with [[velin-razar]] — something that didn't resolve cleanly, didn't file neatly, and hasn't stopped being interesting. The event is still open. Fiona is probably the one who has done the most follow-up thinking about it.

## Talents

**Doctor's Orders** — When coordinating others or coercing someone into a course of action: use Medicine instead of Command. Req: Medicine 4+.

**Testing a Theory** — If you succeeded at a previous task in this adventure in the same scientific or technological field: the first bonus d20 you buy on an Engineering or Science task is free. **Quick Study** — Ignore all Difficulty increases and complication range increases from unfamiliarity with practices, techniques, procedures, or species. Req: Science 3+ or Medicine 3+.
