---

## Profile

A stable, resource-wealthy single-star system with two habitable worlds in the primary zone — an unusual configuration that gave the Varanid Authority's civilization an exceptional head start. The star's metal-rich composition translates throughout the system: inner rocky worlds heavy with extractable minerals, gas giants with fuel-skimmable atmospheres, and a deep outer belt of cold but workable ice and ore.

The Varanid Authority developed a robust spacefaring culture using hydrogen ramjet technology long before warp drive, building infrastructure across the system over generations. That engineering tradition runs deep. Old ramjet-era infrastructure is still present throughout — fuel depots, relay stations, mining platforms, some still operational, some derelict.

---

## System Structure

**Inner zone (Planets 1–2):** Rocky, mineral-rich worlds. Actively mined.

**Primary zone (Planets 3–4):** Two habitable worlds. One is believed to be Varanidia Prime, the population center. The other is agricultural and provides significant food surplus for the wider Authority network.

**Outer-middle (Planet 5):** Primary gas giant; fuel skimming operations; mining moons.

**Outer zone (Planets 6–7):** Second gas giant (historical ramjet destination, cultural significance); cold rocky/ice world beyond.
