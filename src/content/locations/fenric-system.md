## Profile

The geometry junction of the Persephone Sector — and therefore one of the most strategically significant systems in the region. Both Geometry paths (Path 1 and Path 2) terminate here on opposite sides of the system. Every ship transiting the Persephone Geometry must cross the full width of Fenric's Star space. There is no quiet passage through.

System diameter (heliopause to heliopause): ~11–17 light-hours. At warp 3, a transit takes minutes. At impulse, days. Any technological civilization in-system has full visibility on all transits.

The Fenric are believed to be aware of everything that moves through their system, and have been for a long time.

---

## System Structure

**Fenric I** — Close-orbiting gas giant. Fuel skimming operations; the Fenric's primary in-system fuel source.

**Fenric II** — Class K, harsh inhabited world. Survivable without domes but only just — high radiation, cratered terrain providing natural shelter. Hosts a multi-generational Kheth refugee community (Kheth refugee clans settled here over generations and are now fully integrated into Fenric society). Also home to salvage yards and engineering workshops operating out of derelict Kheth ship hulls.

**Fenric III** — Class K, the main inhabited world. Fenric homeworld. Swamp and fen terrain in lowlands; hills and valleys in highlands. Government seat. Communal architecture predominates — large shared spaces, minimal private quarters.

**Fenric IV** — Gas giant, fuel skimming operations; secondary strategic asset.

**Fenric V** — Outer asteroid belt. Some mining activity.
