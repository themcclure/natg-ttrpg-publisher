# Kelvar System

**Location type:** Star System
**Parent:** [[persephone-sector]]
**Controlling faction:** [[kelvari-reflection]]

**Star:** M3V — Red Dwarf, Main Sequence (warmer and brighter than most M-types in the sector; tidal activity)
**System phenomena:** Asteroid belt (additional mining resource)

**Local name:** Unknown to Federation records
**Dominant species:** Kelvari (Varanid offshoot; see [[kelvari-reflection]])

---

## Profile

Approximately 6 light-years from Varanidia — a deliberate choice by the original colonists, who selected a distance that made return difficult but not impossible. At warp 1 (the capability of the period), that was a six-year voyage.

The system's M3V star drives strong tidal activity throughout. The habitable world is volcanically active in the Icelandic mode: not wall-to-wall devastation, but persistent geothermal energy, mineral-rich hot springs, dramatic terrain, and an atmosphere shaped by centuries of low-level volcanic outgassing. For ectotherms, it is a thermally generous world — ambient warmth from the ground itself.

The asteroid belt adds further extractable resources. Between the geothermal mineral wealth and the belt, the system is resource-rich by sector standards. The Varanid Authority has had commercial interest in the system since re-establishing contact approximately 100 years ago.
