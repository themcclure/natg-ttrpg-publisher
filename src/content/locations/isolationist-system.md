## Profile

A quiet M4V+KV binary, unremarkable on long-range sensors. Five planets, no phenomena. The only notable feature from outside is the heliopause marker network: automated buoys broadcasting in all known sector languages, flagging every arriving vessel before it can enter the system.

The message is the same every time:

> *"We are the Illari. We do not wish to know you, and you may not know us. Leave, immediately."*

No follow-up. No negotiation channel. Ships that don't leave are escorted out — or, reportedly, destroyed.

The Illari are believed to be continuously monitoring their border. They know who arrives, how long they linger, and when they leave.
