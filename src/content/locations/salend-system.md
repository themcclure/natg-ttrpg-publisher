# Salend System

**Location type:** Star System
**Parent:** [[persephone-sector]]
**Controlling faction:** [[salendi]]
**Status:** Active

**Star:** G4VI — Yellow Subdwarf, older and metal-poor, dimmer than a true G-type
**Federation working designation:** Independent Y
**Local name:** Salend (world); Salendis (star, Salendi name)
**Dominant species:** Salendi (human-appearing; do not identify as human)
**Coordinates:** lx 11, ly 12 (Persephone Sector)

---

## Profile

A quiet, unremarkable system — dim star, no phenomena, modest resources. Seven planets; at least one primary zone habitable world. The colony there is pre-warp and appears to have been established long ago, with no remaining space travel capability.

The Salendi are human in appearance. They do not identify as human or claim any off-world origin; they identify entirely by their world's name. The connection between the Salendi and humanity is unverified — the similarity may be coincidence, parallel evolution, or something else. No Federation contact has been established.

The Varanid Authority is believed to have established a commercial presence in the system, likely some form of trade relationship with the colony.
