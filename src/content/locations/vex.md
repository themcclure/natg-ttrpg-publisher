The fourth planet of the [[Vell Prime System]], Vex is a cold, airless rock notable only for the coincidence of its name. Federation surveyors catalogued it before [[Vex]] the bartender was born, which has led to exactly the kind of confusion you'd expect on any manifest that fails to scope its references.
