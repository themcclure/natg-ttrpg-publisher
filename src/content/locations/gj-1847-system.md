## Profile

A binary red-orange dwarf system at the upper-left of the Persephone Sector. The primary is dim, flare-active, and metal-poor — a stingy star that shaped its dominant species through scarcity. What remains of the system's easy resources requires serious engineering effort to access; the Kheth exported that effort outward through military pressure long ago.

From outside, the system looks unremarkable. From inside, it is dense with infrastructure — layered, retrofitted, and entirely functional. The most significant installations are the drydock and cargo hub network on the moons of the outer gas giant — the military-industrial core of the Kheth Confederacy, built over generations.

---

## System Structure

**Planet 1 — GJ-1847 I:** Class B, volcanic world. Mineral-rich but hazardous. Automated extraction platforms.

**Planet 2 — Kheth Prime:** Kheth homeworld. Hills-and-valleys terrain, cave systems, dark radiation-adapted vegetation. The government sits here by convention.

**Planet 3 — GJ-1847 III:** Outer gas giant; fuel skimming. The moons host the Confederacy's drydocks — oldest continuously-inhabited Kheth structures outside the underground cities of Kheth Prime.
