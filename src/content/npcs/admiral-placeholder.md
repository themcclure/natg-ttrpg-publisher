Admiral Kira Placeholder commands Starfleet operations across the [[Rhegion Sector]] from her office at [[Starbase Kappa]]. She issued the orders that dispatched the [[uss-testament]] and its crew — including [[commander-val-thelor|Commander Thelor]] — to investigate reports of [[Kappa Syndicate]] activity at [[The Wayward Comet]].

Placeholder has a reputation for decisiveness and an unshakeable faith in the judgement of [[Captain Missing]], whose current whereabouts are not publicly acknowledged.
