# Gareth Desar

**Role:** Director, [[outpost-428]]
**Faction:** [[federation]] (Federation Office of Regional Administration)
**Location:** [[outpost-428]]
**Status:** Active

---

## Profile

Veteran diplomat, late career. Appointed to Outpost 428 by FORA approximately five years ago, now in his final year of a standard six-year rotation. Presents as human. Competent, unhurried, and accustomed to the particular isolation of a posting 85 days from Command by dispatch.

He was at post for the entire Dominion War — receiving it through dispatches with no live contact from Command, watching the Federation nearly come apart from the edge of known space. His family survived intact. He has been quietly counting down since.

He trusts his in-person read over any file. First meetings are observational — he is calibrating, not performing. He came up on patience and judgment, with no notable family name to leverage. He doesn't need you to know that.

One year from relief. Whatever is stirred up before then, he will be managing the fallout on his way out.

---

## History

- Approximately five years ago: Posted to Outpost 428 by FORA, following the recall of his predecessor (a joined Trill, recalled for Dominion diplomatic expertise). The handover was compressed to a few weeks.
- 2373–2375: At post throughout the Dominion War. Dispatches only; no live contact with Command.
- 2375: War ends. Contacted family. All survived.
- Mid-2377: New overnight crew (the PCs) arrived. One year remaining in his rotation.
