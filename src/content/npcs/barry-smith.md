# Barry Smith

**Role:** Chief of Security, [[outpost-428]]
**Location:** [[outpost-428]]
**Status:** Active

---

## Profile

Chief of Security on Outpost 428. Civilian appointment under his own charter — he doesn't rotate, doesn't report to Starfleet, and successive Directors have learned to work with his operational independence rather than against it. He has been Chief for six to eight years and on the outpost itself for over a decade.

He is not what security chiefs are supposed to look like. Mid-forties in appearance, open and apparently at ease, the kind of face that is easy to underestimate. First names as a matter of course. Offers coffee or a read on the situation before he asks for anything. When he talks at length, he is being careful with you.

What he actually is, is the best-read person on the outpost. He knows faces. He knows shipping manifests against declared routes. During the Dominion War, when Starfleet pulled back and the pocket empires started testing what they could get away with, Barry expanded Security's reach into the broader sector — cultivating information sources and ensuring the vacuum didn't become an invitation. He did this without fanfare and without additional staff.

He has been offered other postings. He has not taken them. He doesn't explain that either.

## History

- Approximately ten or more years ago: Arrived on Outpost 428.
- Approximately six to eight years ago: Promoted to Chief of Security early in the previous Director's rotation.
- 2373–2375: Dominion War. Expanded Security's operational reach into the sector.
- Mid-2377: New crew rotation arrives. Processes them through customs personally.
